iOS Development for AI-Powered Products

We have been shipping iPhone applications since the day the App Store launched. Over the last 16 years, we have partnered with early-stage startups, growth companies, and major financial institutions.

Our specialty is greenfield development: taking a product from concept to launch while building the engineering culture and team that will carry it forward. We are particularly well-suited for AI-integrated products, bringing both deep technical expertise and a practical understanding of the regulatory landscape, including the EU AI Act and its implications for products shipping internationally.

We do not just write code. We establish architecture, hire and mentor your team, and create the foundations for long-term success.

Services

0 to 1: Launch and Team Construction

Best for startups with product-market fit but no mobile presence. We take full ownership of your iOS product launch, laying the technical foundation with modern, scalable architecture and establishing your CI/CD, security, and release pipelines. As we build your app, we hire and train your permanent in-house team, ensuring a seamless handover when you are ready to fly solo.

1 to N: Optimization and Auditing

Best for established teams facing technical debt or growth pains. We provide high-level architectural oversight, audit existing codebases for security and performance, implement AI-assisted workflows to boost developer productivity, and mentor your mid-level developers into seniors.

AI Integration Advisory

Building an AI-powered iOS app brings unique challenges: on-device versus cloud inference, privacy considerations, and an evolving regulatory environment. We help you make informed architectural decisions and understand compliance requirements before they become expensive problems.
